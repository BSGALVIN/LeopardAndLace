About Us

Leopard & Lace is an Australian business located in Morisset (near Newcastle, NSW).This business was established in December 2010.

Our range includes Corsets, Lingerie, Costumes, Retro Clothing & Accessories.

We have a strong customer service focus and have strict procedures for the quality control of our products set in place to ensure that you receive a quality item. ALL of our corsets are check measured to ensure they are within the measurements range shown in our size chart. So you can have confidence knowing that the garment you order will have the same waist measurements that are shown in the chart. If you have any questions or need help with sizing, please give us a call for assistance.
